## Active Projects

### 🛡️ [Halal-AI-Auditor — MACI Framework](https://github.com/Beenishfatima1472/Halal-AI-Auditor)
Real-time AI output auditing framework. Five-pillar, 50-point compliance scoring across Faith, Life, Intellect, Lineage, and Property dimensions. Validated on GPT-4o shadow audit (26/50, Not Certified). Core classifier achieves F1 0.87, recall 100% on adversarial suite of 55 cases across Arabic, English, Arabizi, Urdu, and code-switching.

`Python` `Scikit-learn` `Streamlit` `NLP` `Apache-2.0`

### 🔬 [MACI Auditor App](https://github.com/Beenishfatima1472/MACI-Auditor-App-Deploy)
Live Streamlit deployment of the MACI auditing tool. Real-time violation detection for Riba, Gharar, Maysir, Fake Fatwa, Hadith Fabrication, Scholar Misquotation, and MLM patterns. Publicly accessible — no sign-up required.

`Streamlit` `Python` `ML Classifier` `Production`
